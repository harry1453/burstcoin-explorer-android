Burstcoin Explorer Privacy Policy

Burstcoin Explorer stores the following information locally on your device (this data is not transmitted):

  • The Account ID, the account balance and the account username (All of which are obtainable on the Burstcoin public ledger) of any accounts that you "pin" within the app
  • The address of the Burstcoin node you are using to interact with the Burstcoin public ledger
  • The currency you have chosen to view the current Burstcoin price in

      • Burstcoin Explorer may transmit the following information:

      • Crash reports via Fabric Crashlytics, which will include information about a crash and system information such as Device Model, Operating System Version. This is to improve your experience using Burstcoin Explorer.

      No other information is collected.
